About Me

My name is Hayley, and I am a programmer.

I have been programming for a few years now and have worked in Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript/jQuery, SQL, PHP and Java. My first language was Python, and is still my preferred language - although I will admit, HTML/CSS is growing on me.

Below is a brief description of my work, if you would like to view my official CV, please do not hesitate to contact me.
